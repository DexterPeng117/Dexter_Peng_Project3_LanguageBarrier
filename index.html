The topic focuses on language barriers in travel and how they affect communication and confidence in real travel situations.

It comes from my experience traveling and facing language problems. It is for travelers, international students who may struggle with communication abroad, and local people.

At the following pages, I will show some details and give tips to help you deal with language barriers while traveling.
